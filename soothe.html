SOOTHE

Soothe is a fictitious brand that serves to provide a safe and healthy alternative to cough drops for children. Most cough drops on the market are full of sugar and are a choking hazard for children. Soothe is a cough lollipop that is safe for children to consume. It's made with all natural ingredients such as herbs and honey to soothe sore throats and fight coughs.


I designed the logo and visual identity for Soothe, including the business system and website.
