SOOTHE

Soothe is a fictitious brand that serves to provide a safe and healthy alternative to cough drops for children since most cough drops on the market are full of sugar and are a choking hazard for children. Instead, Soothe is a cough lollipop that is safe for children to consume. It is made with all natural ingredients such as herbs and honey to soothe sore throats and fight coughs.

I designed the logo and visual identity for Soothe, including the business system and website.
